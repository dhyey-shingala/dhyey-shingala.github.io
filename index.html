My Philosophy

I believe in writing code that stands the test of time. Software that doesn't rely on external frameworks is inherently more robust, as it remains functional even as specific functions or methods may become deprecated. This approach ensures longevity and sustainability, allowing the software to work indefinitely.

Additionally, I advocate for a minimal aesthetic. I prioritize functionality over frills and glitter, believing that simplicity enhances user experience and maintains focus on what truly matters. Clean, efficient design is not just visually appealing; it also fosters clarity and usability.

Furthermore, I emphasize the importance of simplicity in software development. Avoiding complexity is crucial; I strive to keep everything straightforward and easy to understand. By doing so, I ensure that everyone, regardless of their technical background, can comprehend and engage with the software effectively.
