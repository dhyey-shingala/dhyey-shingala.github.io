About Myself

Hi, I am Dhyey. I graduated with a masters in Computer Science from Texas Tech University in May 2023. I like to keep learning, in my spare time I always try to see what new things are happening in the Tech industry. I am a foodie and like to try out and experiment with different ingredients.

My journey in tech started back in India where I did my bachelors. I developed my first full stack app in ASP.NET during that time. I liked the idea of a full stack developer you get to experience all facets of an application and makes you much more nuanced when it comes to making decisions regarding which stack would be better for a specific app or just being able to understand bugs better. During my bachelors I was also working in ASWDC which was a part of the institute that allowed students to build industry-level projects from scratch. In my time there I learnt ASP.NET, Angular, C#, Typescript and SQL.

After graduating with my bachelors I had already made plans to come to the US to pursue a masters degree, I was working as a full stack developer at Setu Consulting which is an IT company in my hometown. There I learned how to work in a team and how to communicate with clients. The team there was amazing and I enjoyed working there. Then in August 2021 I came to the US to pursue a masters degree in computer science. During the two years I learnt python, machine learning, software security and worked in group projects with a lot of really talented peers. The experiences that I have had here really helped me grow and become a better person and a better developer. Looking forward to what the future has in store for me.
